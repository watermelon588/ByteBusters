📌 What We Do

At JeevaCare, we believe that quality healthcare is not a privilege, but a basic right for everyone, especially the backbone of our society – industrial workers and underserved communities.

We provide an integrated platform that connects workers, NGOs, and doctors through:

  • 📱 Telemedicine – Video consultations with doctors anytime.
  • 👩‍⚕️ Women’s Health Tools – Period & hygiene tracking for better awareness.
  • 🚑 Emergency Care – Quick ambulance and emergency doctor access.
  • 🤝 NGO Partnerships – Awareness camps and free medical check-ups.

🌍 Benefits to Society

🏥 Affordable Healthcare

Industrial workers can access doctors without financial strain, reducing out-of-pocket expenses.

👩 Women Empowerment

Our tools empower women to take control of their reproductive and mental health with dignity.

🚑 Immediate Response

Emergency services prevent loss of lives due to delays in medical attention.

📢 Community Awareness

Health camps spread vital knowledge on hygiene, nutrition, and preventive care.

💡 How It Works

1

Factories & NGOs partner with JeevaCare to bring healthcare to workers.

2

Workers Register on our platform via mobile or NGO help desks.

3

Healthcare Access – workers use telemedicine, health tracking & NGO services.

4

Emergency Support is dispatched instantly when required.

5

Impact Measurement – NGOs & JeevaCare track health outcomes for improvement.

📊 Social Impact

JeevaCare is not just about healthcare—it’s about building a healthier society. Every worker we support leads to a stronger family, a more productive workforce, and a brighter future for the community.

  • ✅ Reduced absenteeism in factories due to health issues.
  • ✅ Improved maternal & child health in worker communities.
  • ✅ Awareness drives reduce disease outbreaks & poor hygiene.
  • ✅ Emergency services save lives every single day.
